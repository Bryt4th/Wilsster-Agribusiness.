Supply of Breeding Stock

We are the leading supplier of quality breeding stock for goats and dairy cows in the region. Our animals are carefully selected for superior genetics, health, and productivity.

Goats

We supply high-quality breeding goats suitable for both meat and milk production. Our goats are disease-resistant and adapted to local conditions.

Dairy Cattle

Our dairy cattle breeding stock comes from superior genetic lines with proven milk production records. We also provide slaughter animals upon prior notification.

We can arrange delivery to your farm and provide guidance on proper animal care and management.

Contract / Partnership Farming

We acknowledge that a large proportion of Uganda's population is youthful, often lacking land for farming but interested in agricultural ventures. At the same time, older landowners may have resources but lack modern farming knowledge.

We bridge this gap by offering transparent, credible and reliable partnership farming options. Our programs can operate at our farms or at clients' farms, with clear terms and shared benefits.

  • Landowner partnerships (we manage, you profit)
  • Investor partnerships (you invest, we manage)
  • Youth empowerment programs
  • Joint venture arrangements
Learn About Partnership Options

Farm Visits, Tours & Agricultural Events

We organize all types of farm-related events within and outside the country to bridge the knowledge and information gap in the agricultural sector.
