Our Agricultural Projects

Explore our current and upcoming initiatives in the agricultural sector.

Current Projects

Goat Farming

Our goat farming project focuses on breeding high-quality goats for both meat and milk production. We maintain a herd of superior genetic stock and supply breeding goats to farmers across the region.

  • Disease-resistant breeds adapted to local conditions
  • Focus on both meat and dairy goat varieties
  • Training programs for goat farmers
  • Breeding stock supply chain
  • Value addition initiatives
Learn More

Dairy Farming

Our dairy farming initiative aims to improve milk production in the region through superior breeding stock, modern farming techniques, and comprehensive farmer support programs.

  • High-yield dairy cattle breeding program
  • Artificial insemination services
  • Milk collection and cooling centers
  • Farmer training in herd management
  • Milk processing and value addition
Learn More

Upcoming Projects

Turkey Rearing

We are expanding our portfolio to include turkey production, responding to growing demand for poultry products in the region. This project will include breeding, rearing, and processing facilities.

  • Introduction of high-quality turkey breeds
  • Modern rearing facilities
  • Processing and value addition
  • Market development initiatives
  • Farmer outgrower programs
